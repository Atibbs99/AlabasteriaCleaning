Why Choose Us?

At Alabasteria Cleaning Services, we know that maintaining a clean building isn’t just about appearances — it’s about protecting your business.

We’re a family-owned company that takes every job seriously because we know what it’s like to build something from the ground up.

We’ve cleaned offices, schools, and worked with large organizations like the YMCA and local car dealerships — places that demand strict attention to detail, fast-paced cleaning, and total trust.

Why businesses choose us:

  • Cleaner spaces mean fewer accidents. A well-maintained facility helps reduce slip and fall risks — protecting your people and your bottom line.
  • Healthier environments mean fewer sick days. Regular disinfecting keeps viruses at bay, so your staff stays healthier and your operations keep moving.
  • A clean building reflects professionalism. Clients, customers, and employees notice — and it shows you care about your business.
  • Clean buildings cost less to maintain. Dirt and buildup can wear down surfaces fast — we help prevent costly repairs with consistent care.

We don’t cut corners — we clean them. And because we’re a family business, you’ll never feel like just another account. We’re reliable, flexible, and genuinely care about your space.

If you want a cleaning team that’s experienced, trustworthy, and invested in your facility’s quality — we’re here for you, so you can focus on what you do best and leave the cleaning to us.

“Because clean should never be a question.”
