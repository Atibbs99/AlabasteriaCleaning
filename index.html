About Us

Founded in 2024, Alabasteria Cleaning Services is a family-run company with deep roots in the cleaning industry. Inspired by our mother’s dedication and honest work, we built this business to deliver reliable, caring service for every client — whether we’re cleaning an office, a school, or a retail space. Our mission is simple: to create cleaner, healthier, more welcoming spaces for the people who use them every day.
